Tashkent – Samarkand – Bukhara

Discover Uzbekistan’s imperial cities in comfort and style with curated experiences and expert guides.

From $730
4 days / 3 nights

Overview

Explore the vibrant capital Tashkent, the blue-domed majesty of Samarkand, and the living museum city of Bukhara. This luxury journey blends cultural depth with refined accommodations and seamless logistics.

Highlights

  • Registan Square and Gur-Emir Mausoleum
  • Siab Bazaar tastings and artisan workshops
  • Historic Bukhara old town and Lyabi-Hauz
  • High-speed Afrosiyob train experience

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 — Tashkent: Old City, Chorsu Bazaar, modern boulevards, welcome dinner.
  2. Day 2 — Samarkand: Registan, Shah-i-Zinda, Gur-Emir, paper-making workshop.
  3. Day 3 — Bukhara: Poi-Kalyan ensemble, Ark Fortress, tea house break.
  4. Day 4 — Bukhara to Tashkent: Free time, train return, departure.
