Aral Sea Expedition

Witness the haunting beauty of the Aral Sea region with expert-led exploration.

From $890
3 days / 2 nights

Overview

Travel across stark desert landscapes to former port towns and ship graveyards. This thoughtful expedition balances comfort with meaningful storytelling.

Highlights

  • Moynaq ship cemetery
  • Karakalpakstan art and culture
  • Sunrise over the Ustyurt Plateau

Itinerary

  1. Day 1 — Nukus: Savitsky Museum, drive to Moynaq.
  2. Day 2 — Aral Basin: Ship cemetery, plateau viewpoints, camp night.
  3. Day 3 — Return: Breakfast in camp, return to Nukus.

Included

  • 4x4 transport and driver
  • Guide and camp equipment
  • Meals as per itinerary

Not Included

  • Flights and visas
  • Personal insurance
  • Tips and personal expenses

Optional Add-ons

  • Helicopter flyover of the Aral basin (subject to weather)
  • Private astronomy session in desert camp
